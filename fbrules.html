Facebook Rules

  1. Can I recover my Facebook password without an email and phone number?

    2. Go to “Login and Password” on the left side menu. Click on “Fix a Login Problem”. On the page, click on “Recover Your Account If You Can't Access the Email or Mobile Number on the Account”. Follow the prompts based on your individual circumstance.

  2. Why can't I reset my Facebook password?

    3. Unable to reset password because of password reset limit

    In order to keep your account safe, we only let you request a new password a certain number of times each day. If you've reached this limit, you'll need to wait 24 hours before trying to reset your password again.
